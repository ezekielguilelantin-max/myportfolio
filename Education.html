Collection Purposes


I was born in Pampanga in 2008, but my life changed when my family moved to Quezon City. Growing up in a new city was challenging, but I quickly adjusted. I started my education at Quirino Elementary School, where I studied from kindergarten to Grade 6. That school became my second home, and I made a lot of memories and friends. Those years helped build the foundation of my learning and confidence.

After elementary, I transferred to Don Quintin Calderon High School for Grades 7 and 8. The experience was different, and I learned to adapt to new environments and people. Then I moved to Tierra Monte School for Grade 9, and later transferred to NCBA for Grade 10. Unfortunately, I had to stop studying for one year, which was a hard time for me. But I didn’t give up because I knew education is important for my future.

Now, I am continuing my journey at Quirino High School, and I chose the TVL ICT strand. I chose this because I really want to learn more about computers and technology. I believe that learning ICT will open many opportunities for me in the future. I’m excited to explore skills like programming, troubleshooting, and web development. This is my story, and it taught me the value of perseverance, growth, and following what I’m passionate about.
